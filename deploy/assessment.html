  • Mission statement:

    Things I Tried is a website dedicated to documenting Caitlin Macleod's experimental approach to trying new/strange things for the eyes of people who like to experiment in their everyday life and want a place to share their experiments with others as well as finding inspiration. Unlike competing sites, which often get over-ridden with meaningless mimicry called memes, Things I Tried will keep its content niche and focused.

    In an ideal world there would be on-site engagement through comments, forums and profiles but as long as I'm hosting this in a static way, I'll have to live with providing ways for fans to share posts to Twitter, Facebook and maybe Pinterest. If I end up hosting this I'd also have unobtrusive advertising to pay for the hosting or even just make the hosting slightly cheaper.

    Goals:

    • Entertainment
    • Information
    • Engagement
    • Advertising
